Burpees

If you want to include burpees in your workout routine you need to focus on maintaining perfect form. Keep your back straight and your core engaged at all times and maintain a natural and regular breathing pattern throughout the exercise.

3 to 5 sets of 8 to 15 burpees
Target Muscle:
Instructions:
1. Stand straight with your feet shoulder-width apart.
2. Squat and place your hands in front of your feet.
3. Jump back until your legs are fully extended and your body is in plank position.
4. Do a push up, jump forward, and then push through the heels to return to the starting position. Repeat until set is complete.
Benefits: Burpees are an intense full body exercise that increases heart rate, strength, agility, and flexibility. This exercise is one of the most complete since it uses all major muscle groups and burns a ton of calories in a short period of time.
